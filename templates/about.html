About

Our Story

Born out of the need to reliably find high quality masks in one place and a conversation with my lovely Grandma who makes a variation of the masks on this website. We decided to do it and solve the problem ourselves. Over the course of several months we developed our service and sources to reliably bring you a wide range of facial masks that aren’t subjected to the absolutely evil price ballooning that we’ve found during this unfortunate crisis.

Any questions? Let us know in by emailing us at asturgeon190@gmail.com or call us at 1-(573)-660-1041

Our Mission

At the Mask Store, our goal isn't to sell masks. It's to save people from the corporate evil that is taking advantage of people during this crisis. We make just enough money to pay our staff and cover expenses. Here at the Mask Store we've seen the exploitation of people too many times in our personal life so we sought out to stand up and deliver masks to people in need, around the globe.
